# Hello! I'm Trenton Peazant.

I am a student at **William & Mary** in Williamsburg, VA, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in **Economics** with a minor in **Data Science**. My academic journey is driven by a passion for understanding complex systems, from urban economics to the mechanics of generative AI.

## What I Do

### Academics & Research
My coursework spans Cross-Section Econometrics, Data Structures, and Computational Problem Solving. I currently serve as a **Qualitative Analyst at DisInfo Lab**, a student think tank where I research and publish reports on domestic and foreign disinformation issues.

### Leadership & Community
I am the **President of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated**, where I oversee community service events and coordinate with members across the country to drive positive impact. Additionally, I work with the **William & Mary Alumni Association** to bridge the gap between students and alumni through mentorship and networking events.

### Athletics
I am a **Varsity Gymnast at William & Mary**. Athletics has taught me the value of dependability and handling pressure—qualities recognized by my teammates with the "Rock Award" in 2023. I have also been honored as a Collegiate Scholar Athlete and an Academic All-American.

### Technical Projects
I enjoy building tools that explore the intersection of data and language. I am currently working on a **Small Language Model** using Python and TensorFlow to deepen my understanding of GPT-like architectures.
